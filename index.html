    WHO WE ARE

  • NEW EDGE INTERNATIONAL is one of the leading Government approved Manpower Agencies in Bangladesh with License No. RL-703, registered under the Ministry of Expatriatesâ€™ Welfare & Overseas Employment which facilitates professionally managed outflow of the workforce from Bangladesh to overseas destinations. NEI is a member of the Bangladesh Association of International Recruiting Agencies (BAIRA).


    In spite of numerous recruitment agencies in Bangladesh, NEI realized that there was a need for a professionally managed recruitment agency to bridge the gap between opportunities and the opportunity seekers and thus the company came into existence. We have experienced staff members with extensive working knowledge of the international industries. We mainly place job seekers in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates, Malaysia, Oman, Qatar, & Bahrain . However, we are now gradually expanding our client base to other Gulf countries such as Qatar, Oman and Bahrain and so on.
